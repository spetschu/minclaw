# Simple Pasta

## Ingredients

- 200g dried pasta
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- salt and black pepper
- basil (optional)

## Steps

1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
2. Warm olive oil in a pan and cook garlic briefly.
3. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
4. Toss in cooked pasta and mix until coated.
5. Finish with basil and extra olive oil.

## Notes

- add chili flakes for heat
- works well as a quick weeknight meal
