# Chess Openings Notes

## Goals

- build a reliable opening repertoire
- focus on principles over memorizing long lines

## White

- Italian Game: active piece development and king safety
- Queen's Gambit: central pressure and strategic play

## Black vs 1.e4

- Caro-Kann: solid pawn structure, fewer tactical landmines

## Black vs 1.d4

- Queen's Gambit Declined: stable setup and clear plans

## Practice Plan

- review one model game per opening each week
- practice first 8-10 moves with ideas, not just move order
